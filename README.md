# Tilly - Legal

This repository hosts the public legal documents for Tilly, a calm,
private pregnancy and early-parenthood companion app for Australian
families, published by Tilly Health.

## Contents

- **privacy.html** - Tilly's Privacy Policy

## About Tilly

Tilly keeps your information on your own device. It does not collect,
transmit, or sell your personal data, and it contains no ads. Tilly
provides general information only and is not a medical service.
